The Spectrum Guessing Game

How to Play:

  1. Two teams take turns. One player sees a spectrum with a hidden target.
  2. That player gives a clue to their team about where the target is.
  3. The team clicks on the spectrum to guess the target's position.
  4. Points are awarded based on accuracy: Perfect (5pts), Good (3pts), Poor (1pt).
  5. First team to reach 10 points wins!
